🎯 The Challenge

The VER & FinOps team manages a $295M portfolio of vendor engagements across Salesforce DET — spanning software, contractors, hardware, and professional services. Leadership needed a single, always-current view of savings performance, contract status, SLA health, and strategic direction — without relying on engineering resources, a dedicated BI team, or a weeks-long build cycle.

The existing approach — spreadsheets, static decks, and ad-hoc reporting — made it impossible to present a coherent, real-time picture to SVP, EVP, and President-level leadership on demand. The goal was to change that, fast.

The Mandate

Build an executive-grade, interactive intelligence platform from raw Coupa export data — visually polished, fully editable, printable, and deployable as a live website — without a single line of engineering support. Delivered in 3–5 days.
