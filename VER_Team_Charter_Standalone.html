What is VER?
VER — Vendor Engagement & Reliability — is Salesforce DET's function responsible for the full lifecycle of vendor engagements: from pre-contract evaluation through active SLA governance, cost optimization, M&A vendor transitions, and strategic roadmap alignment. FinOps discipline runs as a process lens across all VER activity — embedded in how every team member works, not siloed to a single role. VER serves as the connective tissue between DET leadership and its managed portfolio of software, labor, and hardware engagements.
